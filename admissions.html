FAQs

Frequently asked

What ages do you accept?

We welcome children from 3 months through preschool age (typically up to 5 years).

What are your operating hours?

Monday to Friday, 7:00 to 17:30. We are closed on weekends and public holidays.

Do you provide meals?

Yes — nutritious meals and snacks are provided as part of our daily program.

How do you handle settling-in?

We schedule gentle settling-in visits so your child can build trust at their own pace.

Can I tour the facilities first?

Absolutely. Book a visit through this page and we'll arrange a convenient time.
