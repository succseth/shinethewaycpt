Programs

Programs that grow with your child

From cuddly infants to confident preschoolers — every age has a thoughtful plan.

Infants (3 – 2 yrs)

A cozy, secure environment. No more than 6 children per group.

Toddlers (2 yrs – 3 yrs)

Preparing for mainstream. Language, fine motor and social skills. 12 Children per group.

Preschool (3 – 5 yrs)

Early literacy, numeracy, and Module C CAPS Curriculum. Creative arts and confidence for big school.

Holiday Program

Themed weeks of crafts, outdoor play and learning adventures during school holidays.

Music & Movement

Weekly music, rhythm and dance to build coordination and joy.

Early Literacy

Reading rituals, phonics games and storytelling that nurture a lifelong love of books.
