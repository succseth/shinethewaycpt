About Us

Caring, learning & growing — together

Caring for children was and is the passion of owner Melanie. When in Mitchell's Plain, she would notice how children in the early hours would travel with their parents to caregivers. Melanie would approach these parents and would care for them until their transport could collect by her. Years later, she would extend the same to neighbors in the Plumstead area. In 2006, she opened Shine the way to do after care seeing that she moved opposite St Anne's. Since then, Shine the way grew from what she intended to a daycare for babies and toddlers.

Our story

For over 20 years we've cared for families across Plumstead and Cape Town. From our very first day, we set out to create a daycare where every child feels seen, valued and inspired.

Today, we proudly continue that mission with qualified staff, a thoughtful curriculum and warm partnerships with parents.
